This is the OnCall dashboard. It’s a central place to organize your team's planning for the next period.

Stay in control with minimum effort. To get started, send planning invites to all team members with a single click. Each team member will receive an email with a unique link through which they can supply their preferences. Think of Doodle. Track the progress or send reminders only to the team members that respond slowly. Share the final schedule with the team with a single click. Team members will again receive a unique link where they can see their assignments and directly save them into their calendar. Splan OnCall brings the right information at your fingertips at the right time.

After we've build your planning, the Splan Score will tell you how good the planning is and you can act on it instantly.

Good days, bad days. Splan OnCall has an intuitive interface to define good and bad days, and we will spread these as good as possible across team members. Because being OnCall on a Saturday is likely to be worse than Thursday, and assigning a team member to work on January 1th is not a great New Year’s gift. Still someone has to do it.

A schedule comes with a Splan Score and other statistics that show you good and fair the planning is. 90 is great. 50 is bad. We’re fan of keeping things simple.
